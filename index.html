About me

Hello! I'm Abdo, a freelance graphic designer. I'm originally from Morocco, but I've lived in the United States for about 5 years now. I've always been passionate about designing and creating visually appealing work. My goal is to help people create beautiful and meaningful designs for their businesses and personal projects. Some of my skills include:

  • Logo design
  • Social media posts
  • Ad designs
  • Thumbnail creation
  • Business cards
  • Photo editing
  • Typography

Why choose me?

I believe that every great designer has a unique voice and style. Abdo has a knack for capturing the essence of different cultures and their unique customs. I'm eager to collaborate with people who share my love for design and my passion for helping others create beautiful and meaningful work.
